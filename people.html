People at Hot Beans Web



Michelle Adams

Michelle is a technologist who co-founded the Mozilla project and created the JavaScript programming language. She also is a key founder in the metaverse and a major investor in crypto currency.

Michael Bisping

Michael is a engineer and a computer scientist who, whilst working at CERN in the early 1990’s, created HTML and thereby effectively the World Wide Web.

Rachel Zane

Rachel is a web designer, web developer, and an assistant researcher at MIT. She is a passionate advocate for web standards and one of the few “Invited Experts” in the W3C CSS.

Walter Lewis

Walter is a web pioneer and web standards activist. Whilst working at CERN in the early 90’s he was confounded by how little control website creators had over the way their websites looked.
